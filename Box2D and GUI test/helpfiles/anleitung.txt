Welcome to "Hole in One", this unique physic game will be your perfect companion for diverting hours.
The aim is to lead the paperball through different levels to the trash can, to fullfill its purpose of life. To succeed the different tasks there is a wide set of tools ready to be used by the player. These tools are necessary to bypass different and many evil obstacles, which will do everything to prevent the ball from reaching his goal.
Be the chosen one and support the paperball!
